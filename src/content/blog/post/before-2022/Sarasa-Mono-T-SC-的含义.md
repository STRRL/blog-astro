Sarasa Mono T SC 作为现在的主力字体, 每次都忘记这几个是啥意思.

sarasa 更纱 Mono 等宽 SC 简体中文.

关于 T:

> Have ligature, Em dashes (——) are full width —— MonoT
> Have ligature, Em dashes (——) are half width —— Mono
> No ligature, Em dashes (——) are half width —— Term
